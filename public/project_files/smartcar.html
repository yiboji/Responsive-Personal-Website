Intelligent Auto-racing Car

Aug.2012 · The 7th "Freescale Cup" Intelligent Car Competition

I led a 3-person team for the Freescale Cup Intelligent Car Competition and built a model racecar to race autonomously around a track for speed. We designed the PCBs for the motherboard, laser detector board, and High-Power motor driver board. We then assembled all the components on the car, coded in C for the ARM processor, and improved the algorithm to maximize speed and stability. I also wrote a paper for one of the innovation methods used in the car.
The video shows how was the car running in the final competition of Anhui division. The car was running at a speed of 2.4M/S or so. However, the speed was mainly limited by the unsmooth racing path in the cross track and the differential mechanism of rear wheels. I later remodeled the cross track as a linear equation related to the width of cross, and improve the racing strategy in algorithm. In the national final, the car could run up to 2.6M/s and much more smooth than this video.
