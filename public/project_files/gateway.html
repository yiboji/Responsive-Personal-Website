Industrial Network Gateway

Nov.2013 ·

I implemented this industrial network gateway for Jifei automation company in China, a real commercial product. I designed the whole system including more rigid hardware design, such as reducing power disturbance, impedance matching and shielding, and programmed part of the slave stacks for CANopen and Profibus.
The first two photos are the PCB which were designed and solded by myself. The third one is the PCB layout, a two-layer PCB. The last one is the whold system, in which the gateway acted as a protocol translator for the Profibus and the CANopen network.
